About Me

For the last thirteen years, I have been working to physically and culturally connect the world by designing, testing, and certifying airplanes at Boeing. In my last role at the Boeing Company, I served as the technical lead for the Folding Wingtip System function. My responsibilities included actuation and control of this new and novel system. I took the idea through the long, arduous process to be ready and safe for first flight. While introducing the first folding wing on a commercial airplane was fun and rewarding, it took 7 years.

I chose to learn a new skill to challenge myself and maintain a leading technology position inthe job market. The field of software development intrigues me because of its fast turn aroundand shorter schedules. Creating and producing things is enjoyable and fulfilling. I want tolive the product lifecycle on a scale that is shorter than a decade. Not to mention, theability software possesses to scale and affect so much of the world comes with a lot of power toimprove the world.
